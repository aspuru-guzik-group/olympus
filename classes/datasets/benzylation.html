N-benzylationÂ¶

This dataset reports the yield of undesired product (impurity) in an N-benzylation reaction. Four conditions of this reaction performed in a flow reactor can be controlled to minimize the yield of impurity. 1

The dataset includes 73 samples with four parameters and one objective.
