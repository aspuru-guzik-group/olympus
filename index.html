Welcome to Olympus’ documentation!¶

Olympus is a toolkit that provides a consistent and easy-to-use framework to access a number of optimization algorithms (Planners) as well as benchmark surfaces. In addition to analytic benchmark functions (Surfaces), a collection of experimental emulators (Emulators) are provided. These are based on models (Models) trained on experimental datasets (Datasets) from across the natural sciences, including examples from physics, chemistry, and materials science.
